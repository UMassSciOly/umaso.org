Register
Registration for UMASO 2024 is now closed.
The registration fee per team this year will be $40. Teams that have registered before October 20, 2023 at 11:59 PM EST have priority in our maximum team capacity. Teams that wish to register past this deadline are encouraged to fill out the form below.
Following registration closing, all students competing on campus must fill out appropriate liability forms due to the in-person nature of the event. Teams must conform to official Science Olympiad 2024 rules regarding Division C membership and eligibility for invitational tournament participation, including the fulfillment of all state fees prior to competition in the UMASO tournament. Participating teams that fail to send registration payment by November 20th are subject to a $20 late fee.
