Tournament Information

Registration

Registration for the 2025 University of Massachusetts Amherst Science Olympiad tournament is now open! Our Division C tournament will be held on campus on February 15th, 2025.


Registration will close on October 18th. We have a limited number of spots available, and we anticipate these spots will fill up fast, so we would recommend registering as soon as possible. Teams will be notified of their acceptance on October 22nd, and accepted teams must pay the $40 registration fee per team by November 15th to secure their spot.

Register Here!

Tournament Details

Similar to last year's tournament, we will be offering special awards for build events! We hope to encourage creative and innovative builds through these awards. More details on these special awards, along with what events we will be running and our events schedule, will be released at a later date.

Merch

We will be selling UMASO merch again this year! We are currently hard at work designing new merch for this year's invitational. Each year, we do our best to ensure high quality merch that is both comfortable and stylish. Please stay tuned for this year's cool new designs! More information about preordering will be released in the future.
