Tournament Information

Registration

Registration for the 2026 University of Massachusetts Amherst Science Olympiad tournament is open! Our Division C tournament will be held on campus on February 14th, 2026.


Registered teams must pay the $40 registration fee or request an extension by November 20th. Teams that do not do so will be subjected to a $70 late registration fee per team instead of the normal $40 fee.


To attend the tournament, please fill out the registration form below:

Register here!

Tournament Details

We will be running the same 21 events as the Massachusetts State Science Olympiad, which includes all main national events excluding Flight. Build events will follow the State guidelines, while all other events (including the Hybrid events Machines, Wind Power, and Material Science) will use the National guidelines.

Similar to last year's tournament, we will be offering special awards for build events! We hope to encourage creative and innovative builds through these awards.

Regarding logistical details (parking, arrival times, event schedule -- also displayed adjacent) for the tournament day, please refer to our Invitational Informational Document. Important note: All teams attending the tournament (students and coaches) are required to complete the release forms before the tournament date.

Merch

Our Publicity team has been hard at work this semester creating awesome new designs, and we are thrilled to share these with everyone. This year, our merch includes a crewneck sweatshirt, a hoodie, a T-shirt, and keychains. Please note that we will only be selling merch through pre-orders; we do not anticipate having merch for sale on the day of the tournament.
