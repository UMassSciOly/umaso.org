Tournament Information

Registration

Registration for the 2025 University of Massachusetts Amherst Science Olympiad tournament is now closed. Our Division C tournament will be held on campus on February 15th, 2025.


Registered teams must pay the $40 registration fee or request an extension by November 20th. Teams that do not do so will be subjected to a $70 late registration fee per team instead of the normal $40 fee.

Register Here!

Tournament Details

We will be running the same 21 events as the Massachusetts State Science Olympiad, which includes all main national events excluding Flight. Build events will follow the State guidelines, while all other events (including the Hybrid events Optics, Wind Power, and Material Science) will use the National guidelines.

Similar to last year's tournament, we will be offering special awards for build events! We hope to encourage creative and innovative builds through these awards.

Merch

Our Publicity team has been hard at work this semester creating awesome new designs, and we are thrilled to share these with everyone. This year, our merch includes a crewneck sweatshirt, a tote bag, and stickers.
