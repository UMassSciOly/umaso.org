Volunteer
The UMASO invitational could not run without the help of our generous volunteers! As an invitational that emphasizes the importance of inclusivity, we hope to reach out to as many individuals who wish to participate in our event as possible. Volunteers and event supervisors get an opportunity to visit the UMass campus and build connections with a diverse set of competing teams and the members of our organization.
We are currently looking for volunteers to help out with the tournament! Volunteers may help out in a variety of ways, including proctoring events, grading tests, helping high school students with navigation, and more. All volunteers will receive free bubble tea after volunteering at the tournament!

If you are interested in volunteering, please fill out this sign-up form!
